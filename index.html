What is Lofify?

Lofify is a professional lo-fi music creator that slows down tempo, adds vintage reverb, and applies spatial 3D effects. Perfect for creating lo-fi hip-hop, chill vibes, and nostalgic ambient music.

Best Lo-Fi Practices

For optimal lo-fi results, use high-quality source files. Songs with strong vocals and melodies work best. Start with the Classic Lo-Fi preset and adjust to your vibe.

Frequently Asked Questions

What audio formats are supported?

We support MP3, WAV, OGG, FLAC, and M4A files up to 50MB in size.

Is my audio file uploaded to a server?

No! All processing happens locally in your browser. Your files never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy.

Can I use this for commercial purposes?

You can use the tool freely, but ensure you have the rights to the original audio content.

Why does processing take time?

Audio processing requires significant computation. We apply speed changes, reverb, and quality preservation algorithms to ensure the best output.
