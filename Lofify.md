# Lofify - Lo-Fi Music Creator with Reverb & 3D Effects

Lofify is a professional lo-fi music creator that slows down tempo, adds vintage reverb, and applies spatial 3D effects to your favorite tracks. Perfect for creating lo-fi hip-hop, chill beats, and nostalgic ambient music directly in the browser. [page:1]

---

## Features

- **Lo-fi audio processing**: Slowdown, vintage-style reverb, and immersive 3D effects for classic lo-fi vibes. [page:1]  
- Browser-based interface with drag-and-drop audio upload and file picker support. [page:1]  
- Supports common audio formats: MP3, WAV, OGG, FLAC, and M4A (up to 50MB). [page:1]  
- Demo audio option so users can try the effects without uploading their own file. [page:1]  
- Clear warning to ensure users only upload audio they have rights to use. [page:1]  

---

## Keyboard Shortcuts

- Space: Play / Pause. [page:1]  
- R: Reset. [page:1]  
- D: Download processed audio. [page:1]  
- ← →: Seek backward / forward. [page:1]  
- ↑ ↓: Volume up / down. [page:1]  
- ?: Show all shortcuts. [page:1]  

---

## Getting Started

1. Open Lofify in a modern browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, etc.). [page:1]  
2. Drag and drop an audio file into the drop zone or click “Browse Files” to select one. [page:1]  
3. Adjust the effects to match your preferred lo-fi sound. [page:1]  
4. Use the keyboard shortcuts for faster control while listening. [page:1]  
5. Download the processed audio when you are happy with the result. [page:1]
